Welcome to my personal page! I am a PhD candidate in Economics at Boston University. My research spans the fields of political economy and development economics. My job market paper investigates legitimacy concerns as a driver of electoral fraud in autocracies. Additionally, I am working on projects examining gendered demand for transportation in urban Ethiopia and using social network analysis to evaluate strategic voting in addressing collective action problems. My research employs quasi-experimental methods and includes designing and implementing randomized controlled trials.
