Welcome to my personal page! I am a PhD candidate in Economics at Boston University.

My research spans the fields of political economy and development economics. I use quasi-experimental methods and have experience designing and implementing randomized controlled trials.

My job market paper investigates legitimacy concerns as a driver of electoral fraud in autocracies. My other projects involve a study on gendered demand for transportation in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, and social network analysis to evaluate the effectiveness of strategic voting in solving the collective action problem.
